# Tableau Dashboard Blueprint

## Dashboard 1: Executive Revenue Overview

Purpose: Show overall retail performance and revenue trend.

Recommended views:

- KPI cards:
  - Total Revenue
  - Total Customers
  - Total Invoices
  - Average Invoice Value
  - Cancellation Rate
- Monthly revenue trend
- Revenue by country
- Top 10 products by revenue

Filters:

- Country
- Order Month
- Customer Segment

## Dashboard 2: Customer Segmentation

Purpose: Show customer value groups using RFM analysis.

Recommended views:

- Customer count by RFM segment
- Revenue by RFM segment
- Average recency, frequency, and monetary value by segment
- At-risk high-value customer table

Business story:

> Not all customers should be treated equally. Champions and loyal customers drive repeat revenue, while at-risk customers may need reactivation campaigns.

## Dashboard 3: Product and Country Performance

Purpose: Identify what sells and where revenue is concentrated.

Recommended views:

- Product revenue ranking
- Quantity sold by product
- Country-level revenue and customer count
- Average invoice value by country

## Dashboard 4: Returns and Cancellation Impact

Purpose: Separate healthy revenue from cancellation/return impact.

Recommended views:

- Cancellation trend by month
- Cancellation lines by country
- Products with high cancellation volume
- Net revenue view after cancellations

## Final Story Point

Recommended conclusion:

> The business should protect high-value repeat customers, monitor at-risk customers for reactivation, focus inventory and marketing around top revenue products, and review products/countries with higher cancellation impact.
